Asset Cool + Big Change

Proof of Concept: The Path to Paperless Operations

Primary Objective

To demonstrate that the Big Change platform can serve as a comprehensive, paperless Field Service Management system for Asset Cool, specifically by automating job scheduling, capturing critical on-site data, and providing real-time operational insights to streamline their pylon cleaning and coating workflows.

The 4 Pillars of Success

1

Real-Time Reporting

A live dashboard will display total meters cleaned and coated within a 5-minute delay of job completion, proving real-time data visibility.

2

Seamless Data Import

Successfully import and display all relevant pylon and cable GIS data into the Big Change CRM, proving system compatibility.

3

Video Evidence Capture

Establish a workflow for hyperlinking on-site video footage to a job card, validating the quality and auditing process.

4

Automated Rescheduling

Demonstrate that jobs marked incomplete due to weather automatically create a new job for rescheduling, mitigating operational risk.

Navigating Technical Challenges

Robot Data Integration

Mitigation: Demonstrate API call capability with simulated data to prove the concept while confirming robot API availability with the Asset Cool team.

Complex Scheduling

Mitigation: Focus the POC on a single, well-defined circuit to prove core scheduling logic before scaling to more complex scenarios.

Specific Team Workflows

Mitigation: Build out the team structure using resource groups and team-based assignments to mirror their exact operational hierarchy.
