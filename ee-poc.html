Proof of Concept: A New Workflow for Euro Energy

Visualizing the transformation from a fragmented, multi-system process to a single, unified, and efficient operational platform.

The Current Challenge: A Disconnected Workflow

Euro Energy's operations are spread across multiple platforms, leading to manual data transfer, communication delays, and compliance risks. This fragmented approach creates inefficiencies that hinder scalability.

1. Job Inception

Data entered into "Installer" CRM

2. Scheduling

Appointments manually booked in Google Calendar

3. On-Site Communication

Photos & updates shared in WhatsApp groups

4. Compliance & Submission

Photos manually downloaded, renamed, and packaged

Measuring Success: Quantifiable POC Goals

This POC is designed to achieve specific, measurable improvements across key operational areas. The success of this initiative will be evaluated against five core criteria that directly address Euro Energy's primary pain points.

1. Reduce Photo Processing Time

By automating photo capture, time-stamping, and document packaging, we aim to cut the manual effort required from the submissions team by at least 50%.

2. Achieve Real-Time Visibility

Replace delayed, chaotic communication with instant, auditable digital workflows. The "permission to board/plaster" process becomes a seamless, real-time approval cycle within a single system.

Before: Manual & Delayed

  • Field team waits for WhatsApp replies.
  • Office team inundated with notifications.
  • No clear audit trail of approvals.
  • High risk of missed messages and delays.

After: Automated & Instant

  • Digital request submitted on-site.
  • Instant, targeted alert to the PM.
  • Digital approval is time-stamped.
  • Full audit trail captured on the job record.

3. Digitize On-Site Paperwork

Eliminate the need for printing, manually signing, and transporting paper forms like TV disclaimers and DWP checks by converting them into secure, signable digital worksheets on the mobile app.
