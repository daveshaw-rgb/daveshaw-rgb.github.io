UK Facilities Management Sector

A Comparative Overview of Key Players

At a Glance

The UK's facilities management (FM) landscape is diverse, featuring large multinational corporations and specialized regional firms. This infographic provides a snapshot of seven key companies, comparing their financial scale, workforce size, and operational focus to highlight the sector's dynamic nature. From comprehensive service providers to niche specialists, each company plays a unique role in maintaining the nation's infrastructure.

Company Specializations & Contract Focus

Beyond the numbers, each company has a distinct area of expertise and targets different types of contracts. The following breakdown illustrates the primary services and typical client profiles, showcasing the diverse specializations within the FM sector.

Pinnacle FM Limited

Services: Comprehensive Hard & Soft FM, Cleaning.

Typical Contracts: Large-scale public sector (schools, universities) and private housing (460,000+ homes).

NSS Maintenance

Services: Building Facade, Roof Maintenance, Glazing, Technical Cleaning.

Typical Contracts: Specialized maintenance for commercial buildings using a self-delivered model.

First Response Group Ltd

Services: Integrated Security & FM, Manned Guarding, Cleaning, Landscaping.

Typical Contracts: Clients requiring a combined security and facilities solution, such as corporate and commercial sites.

Sherwoods Ltd

Services: Planned Preventative Maintenance (PPM), M&E, HVAC.

Typical Contracts: Regional specialist for commercial, industrial, and public sector clients in the South West UK.

Rose Property Services

Services: FM Cleaning, Gardening, Window Cleaning.

Typical Contracts: Niche provider for residential blocks of flats and large estates, working with property managers.

DFP Services Ltd

Services: Building Compliance (Fire, Security), Plumbing, Heating, Renewables.

Typical Contracts: Significant presence in the social housing sector with housing associations and councils.

P.F.L. Electrical Ltd

Services: Electrical Installations, Kitchen/Bathroom Refurbishments, Heating.

Typical Contracts: Specialized contractor for social housing and construction on new build and refurbishment projects.
